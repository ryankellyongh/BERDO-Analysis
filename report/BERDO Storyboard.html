07 — Conclusion
Policy recommendations

Three paths to
meaningful emissions reductions

01
Expand technical assistance
Target condominiums, LLCs, and public-sector owners that face fragmented ownership and limited engineering capacity.
02
Simplify the reporting process
Missing data is a structural problem, not random noise. Proactive intervention beats reactive enforcement.
03
Prioritize retrofit for high-EUI buildings
1,003 high-priority buildings combine intense energy use with complex ownership — these need deep decarbonization pathways, not just compliance paperwork.
